My Friends

I have college friends, I have school friends; like any other person. My friends are important because they're the ones that you make the memories with that you wouldnt with your family. I always have a good time with my friends, from going out to dinner to going out on a Friday night. Every fourth of July my friends and I go to a huge beach house and spend the morning, day, and night there playing games and watching fireworks. It is one of my favorite holidays, and is spent with some of those closest to me.
